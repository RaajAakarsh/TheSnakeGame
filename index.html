SnakeMania
1. Use the arrow keys (Up, Down, Left, Right) to control the movement of the snake.
2. Eat the food (displayed as a dot) to grow longer.
3. Avoid colliding with the walls or the snake's own body.
4. Press 'M' to toggle the background music on or off.
5. Each food eaten increases your score.
6. Try to beat the high score possible by surviving as long as you can without colliding.
